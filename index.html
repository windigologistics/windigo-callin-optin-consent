Windigo Logistics SMS Call-In and Communications

Employees may voluntarily opt in to receive SMS text messages from Windigo Logistics for:

  • Call-in confirmations
  • Attendance modifications
  • Payroll reminders
  • Weather alerts
  • Emergency communications
  • Benefits and open enrollment notices

Example - SMS Consent Process

To enroll in Windigo Logistics SMS communications:

  • Text OPTIN to the Windigo Logistics call-in number.
  • Enter your Employee ID.
  • Enter your 4-digit PIN.
  • Review the following SMS consent disclosure:
SMS Consent Disclosure

By replying YES, you agree to receive SMS text messages from Windigo Logistics regarding call-in confirmations, attendance updates, payroll reminders, weather alerts, emergency communications, and benefits/open enrollment notices.

Message frequency varies.

Message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to opt out.

Reply HELP for assistance.

  • Reply YES to confirm enrollment.
