SMS Call-In and Communications

Employees may voluntarily opt-in to receive SMS messages for:

  • Call-in confirmations
  • Attendance modifications
  • Payroll reminders
  • Weather alerts
  • Emergency communications
  • Benefits and open enrollment notices

Employees can opt-in by:

  • Texting START to the company call-in number, or
  • Providing a mobile phone number during onboarding or HR updates

Message frequency varies.

Message and data rates may apply.
